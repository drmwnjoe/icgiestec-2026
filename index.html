About Us

About ICGIESTec 2026

The 2nd International Conference on Global Innovations in Education, Science, and Technology is organized by Universitas Muhammadiyah Mataram as part of its ongoing commitment to advancing international scholarly collaboration and promoting interdisciplinary dialogue in education, science, and technology. This conference is designed to foster synergy among these fields in order to contribute to the development of sustainable and progressive solutions for global challenges. It brings together researchers, academics, and practitioners to share innovative ideas and research findings across five main thematic tracks, namely Educational Science and Technology, which explores innovations in educational technology, artificial intelligence in learning, digital literacy, and the integration of gamification as well as virtual and augmented reality in education; Mathematics and Mathematical Physics, which covers applications of mathematics in science, technology, and industry, including algebra, geometry, number theory, computational mathematics, and both theoretical and experimental physics; Statistical Physics and Nonlinear Systems, which focuses on complex systems, chaos theory, nonlinear dynamics, big data analysis, phase transitions, and emergent phenomena in physical and biological systems; Computational Science, which emphasizes algorithm development, optimization techniques, numerical simulations, machine learning-based computation, quantum computing, and cloud and distributed computing technologies; and Earth Science, Environment, and Energy, which addresses climate change, renewable energy innovations, environmental engineering, disaster monitoring and prediction systems, geophysics, and sustainable earth exploration.

Research Areas

Conference Topics

Explore interdisciplinary research areas in education, science, and technology

1. Educational Science and Technology

  • Innovation in Educational Technology
  • Digital Learning and Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Digital Literacy and Global Communication
  • Gamification and Virtual/Augmented Reality in Education

2. Mathematics and Mathematical Physics

  • Applications of Mathematics in Technology and Industry
  • Topics in Algebra, Geometry, and Real Analysis
  • Number Theory and Computational Mathematics
  • Mathematics in Theoretical and Experimental Physics
  • Mathematical Simulation and Dynamic Systems Modeling

3. Statistical Physics and Nonlinear Systems

  • Complexity in Physical and Biological Systems
  • Statistical Models in Modern Physics
  • Chaos Theory and Nonlinear Dynamics
  • Big Data and Complex Systems
  • Phase Transitions and Emergence in Nonlinear Systems

4. Computational Science

  • Algorithms and Optimization Techniques
  • Numerical Simulation and Modeling
  • AI-Driven and Machine Learning-based Computation
  • Quantum Computing and Its Applications
  • Cloud and Distributed Computing Technologies

5. Earth Science, Environment, and Energy

  • Climate Change and Renewable Energy Innovations
  • Sustainable Technologies for Environmental Solutions
  • Disaster Monitoring and Prediction Systems
  • Environmental Engineering and Green Energy
  • Geophysics and Earth Exploration
